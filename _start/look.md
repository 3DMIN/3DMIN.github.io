A digital-acoustic instrument developed within the [3DMIN project](http://3dmin.org).

PushPull features 

+ a custom-made bellow, 
+ a pair of microphones, 
+ a 6DOF inertial sensor, and 
+ several encoders, buttons and capacitive sensors.
